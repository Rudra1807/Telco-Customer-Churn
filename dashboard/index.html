💡 Actionable Insights
🔥

Fiber Optic Customers Are Churning Fast

Fiber optic subscribers have a ~41.9% churn rate — nearly 3× higher than DSL customers. Competitive pricing or service quality issues should be investigated immediately.

⚡

Month-to-Month Contracts Drive 88% of Churn

Customers on month-to-month contracts churn at 42.7% vs. 11.3% (one-year) and 2.8% (two-year). Incentivizing longer-term plans is the highest-leverage retention lever.

💳

Electronic Check Payers Churn 2× More

45.3% of electronic check users churned vs. ~15–18% for automatic payment methods. Migrating customers to auto-pay could significantly reduce churn.

🛡️

177 Rescue Targets Hold $532K in Recoverable Revenue

High-spend customers (avg $3,010 LTV) at high churn risk represent the best ROI for retention campaigns. Proactive outreach within 30 days is recommended.

📈

Tenure is the Strongest Retention Signal

Churn risk drops from ~60% in the first year to <10% after 5 years. Early-tenure onboarding programs (0–12 months) deliver the highest long-term LTV impact.

🎯

Senior Citizens Churn 41.7% vs 23.6%

Senior customers have 76% higher churn likelihood. Targeted plans with simplified billing, dedicated support, or senior discounts could dramatically improve retention.
